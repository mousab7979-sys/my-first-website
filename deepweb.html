Dark Web

The dark web is a part of the internet that is not accessible through regular search engines
like Google and requires special software to enter. It is designed to provide privacy and
anonymity to its users, which can be helpful for journalists, activists, and people living
under strict censorship. However, the dark web is also known for illegal activities such as
the sale of stolen data, illegal goods, and cybercrime, which makes it risky and unsafe.
Because of these dangers, it is important to understand that the dark web should not be
used casually and is often monitored by law-enforcement agencies.The dark web is a very
